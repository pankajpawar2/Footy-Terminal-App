Rules of the Game
The object of the game is to kick the football between a series of 4 goal posts. 
Depending on which posts the ball goes between will depend on how many points you score. 
The ball can be passed around between team mates in a series of fashions and the winning team will be the team with the highest number of points at the end of the game.

SCORING

If the ball is kicked between the two goal posts (middle posts) then 6 points are awarded. 
If the ball goes between a goal post and one of the behind posts (point) then 1 point is awarded.
